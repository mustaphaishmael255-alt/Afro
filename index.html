Our Story

The Journey in Every Leaf

Afro Tea began with a simple realization: the world’s finest teas grow under the African sun, yet their stories are rarely told by us. We’ve traveled from the misty highlands of Kenya to the golden valleys of the Cederberg to bring you more than just a drink. We bring you a ritual—a piece of the continent’s soul, harvested with respect and poured with pride.

We believe the ritual of tea should be as bold as the land it comes from. Afro Tea is dedicated to sourcing premium, single-origin blends directly from African estates. By honoring the soil and the hands that tend to it, we ensure every cup reflects the vibrant spirit and ancient traditions of our heritage.
